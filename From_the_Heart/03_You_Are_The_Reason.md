“You Are the Reason” was written during a turning point in my life.

I was in a place where I needed direction, purpose, and something to believe in. Meeting her changed the way I saw my future and what I believed I was capable of becoming.

This song is about choosing a different path because someone made you want to be better. She became the reason I woke up differently, thought differently, and tried harder. Not because she asked me to, but because her presence made the choice clear.

Within a few months of meeting her, she was pregnant with Maverick, and everything in my life shifted.

This song captures that moment when one person becomes the reason you stand up, move forward, and start building the life you didn’t know you were ready for.
